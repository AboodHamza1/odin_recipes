Lasagna Recipe !

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Layering Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan

Recipe

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes at 375F (Preheated)
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
